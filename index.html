What pets are there?

There are many different options for pets but for some people different pets are better to get then others. Here are some of the pets that I would recommend for people to get.

  • Fish- these are for people new to pets as they will help you get into the habot of feeding a pet daily
  • birds- these are more of an intermidiate pet, they are good to get and learn how to take care of a pet more then a fish but are not on the same level of commitment as something like a cat or dog
  • cats and dogs- these are pets that are difficult to care for but if you get into a routine they can be very rewarding to have
  • reptiles- reptiles are fun pets to have and some people love to have them

Keep in mind these are only my suggestions for pets and there are many more that you can get so if you are contimplating getting a pet, take a trip to your local pet store and browse arounf for a bit.

Pros and Cons

There are many pros and cons that need to be considered when getting a pet. Here are some that I have thought about in the past when getting pets.

Pros

  • there have been many studies that show pets can help raise your levels of happiness
  • pets are great companions that can help you not feel lonly
  • pets like dogs are great for helping to get you more active
  • pets have also been shown to redice level of stress

Cons

  • pets can be expensive to get and keep healthy
  • pets may be difficult to clean up after

With all of these it may be hard to decide of you do want to get a pet and in my opinion you should they are great companions and are a joy to be around
